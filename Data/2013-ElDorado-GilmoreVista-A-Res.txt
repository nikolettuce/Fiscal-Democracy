RESOLUTION NO. 180-2012

OF THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF THE COUNTY OF EL DORADO

GILMORE VISTA ZONE OF BENEFIT #98122 WITHIN COUNTY SERVICE AREA #9 - SETTING FOR
MAILED BALLOT ELECTION A MEASURE TO ESTABLISH A SPECIAL TAX FOR THE PURPOSES OF
PROVIDING ROAD IMPROVEMENT AND MAINTENANCE SERVICES.

WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors of the County of El Dorado has formed County Service Area #9
pursuant to Title 3, Division 2, Part 2, Chapter 2.2 of the California Government Code; and

WHEREAS, this Board formed Gilmore Vista Zone of Benefit #98122 on July 23, 1985 by Resolution 192-
85 for the purpose of providing road improvement and maintenance services; and

WHEREAS, California Government Code §25215.2, together with §25217.4, expressly authorizes a zone
within a county service area to levy a special tax for road improvement and maintenance services and
drainage facility maintenance services subject to voter approval; and

WHEREAS, the County Registrar of Voters has determined that forty-nine registered voters reside within the
zone of benefit boundaries; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to California State Law, the Board of Supervisors is required to place before the
electorate the approval of the measure as shown on Exhibit A before the special tax can be levied; and

WHEREAS, such a special tax shall take effect only if approved by a two-thirds majority of the voters
casting a ballot in the special tax ballot proceeding; and

WHEREAS, the zone advisory committee has determined a special tax of $270.00 for each improved parcel
and $120.00 for each unimproved parcel is reasonable and sufficient to provide road improvement and
maintenance services within the zone of benefit commencing in fiscal year 2013/2014

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED the Board of Supervisors determines as follows:

1.	The measure as shown on Exhibit A shall be submitted to the voters of the Gilmore Vista Zone
of Benefit #98122 within County Service Area #9.

2.	The El Dorado County Registrar of Voters shall submit the measure as shown on Exhibit A to
the electorate in a mailed ballot election to be held on May 7,2013.

3.	The tax as shown on Exhibit A, if approved, shall appear as a separate item on the tax bill of
each affected parcel and shall be collected at the same time and maimer as county ad valorem
property taxes are collected as set forth in the applicable provisions of the Revenue and
Taxation Code, and shall be subject to the same penalties and the same procedure and sale in
the case of delinquency as provided for such taxes. All laws applicable to the levy, collection
and enforcement of county property taxes shall be applicable to such tax.

4.	If approved, the special tax shall be subject to the accountability measures required by State
Law, including Government Code §50075.1.

5.	The special tax shown on Exhibit A shall take effect and be imposed only if it is approved by a
two-thirds majority of the votes cast in the mailed ballot election to be conducted on May 7,
2013.
