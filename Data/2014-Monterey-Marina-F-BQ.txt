Ballot question

The question on the ballot appeared as:[i]

To continue preserving funds for general city services, including
maintaining firefighters and police officers for adequate emergency
response, reducing crime and criminal gang and drug activity, maintaining 99
city streets and parks, and youth after school programs; shall the City of
Marina adopt an ordinance extending the temporary one cent transactions
and use (sales) tax to automatically expire on March 31, 2026. [2]
