MEASURE N - SPECIAL TAX (SANTA VENETIA - FLOOD ZONE #7

Shall the ordinance of the Board of Supervisors of Flood
Control Zone No. 7 imposing a special tax as described
below be confirmed and the appropriations limit be
increased by an amount equal to the proceeds of said tax
for each of the next four (4) years?

Class I - All property zoned single-family residential -
improved - and agricultural improved -,' shall be taxed
at a rate not to exceed Ten Dollars ($10.00) per month
for the first year .and that each year for the following
three (3) years, this maximum monthly rate may be
increased by One Dollar ($1.00);

Class II - All property zoned multi-family residential
improved, shall be taxed at a rate not to exceed Ten
Dollars ($10.00) per month, per living unit for the first
year, for the following three (3) years, this monthly
maximum rate may be increased by One Dollar ($1.00);

Class III - All property zoned multi-family residential -
unimproved and commercial, shall be taxed at a rate not
to exceed Sixty Dollars ($60.00) per month, per acre,
for the first year, and each year for the following
three (3) years, this maximum monthly rate may be
increased by Six Dollars ($6.00).
