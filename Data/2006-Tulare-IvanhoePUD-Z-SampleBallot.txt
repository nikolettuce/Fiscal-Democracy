IVANHOE PUBLIC UTILITY DISTRICT STREET LIGHTING PROJECT

Shall the Board of Directors of the IVANHOE PUBLIC UTILITY DISTRICT be allowed to establish a community
street lighting project to supplement the existing County sponsored street lighting? The monthly assessment for a
single family resident is proposed to not exceed two dollars ($2.00) per month. The project calls for the addition of 102
lights within the IVANHOE PUBLIC UTILITY DISTRICT.

YES

NO

IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY COUNTY COUNSEL
MEASURE Z

Article XIIID of the California Constitution authorizes a special district to levy assessments, provided that the proper
procedures are followed and provided the assessment is approved by a two-thirds vote of the electorate residing in the
district.

The Ivanhoe Public Utilities District proposes to establish a community street lighting project and has determined that
the monthly assessment for a single family resident to fund the project will not exceed two dollars ($2.00) per parcel
per month.

The measures stated purpose is to establish a street lighting project and provide for the addition of 102 lights within the
Ivanhoe Public Utility District.

The measure makes no change to existing law. The electors entitled to vote on this measure are the registered voters
within the boundaries of the District. If the measure is not approved by at least two-thirds of the votes cast on the
measure, the measure will fail and the assessment will not be implemented.
