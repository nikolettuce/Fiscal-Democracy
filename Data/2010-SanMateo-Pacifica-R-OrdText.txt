ORDINANCE NO. 774-C.S.

AN ORDINANCE OF THE CITY OF PACIFICA AMENDING SECTION 3-7.03
OF THE PACIFICA MUNICIPAL CODE TO INCREASE THE TRANSIENT
OCCUPANCY TAX ON RENT CHARGED TO HOTEL GUESTS FROM TEN
PERCENT (10%) TO TWELVE PERCENT (12%)

The City Council of the City of Pacifica ordains as follows:

Section 1. Section 3-7.03 of the Pacifica Municipal Code, entitled “Tax imposed” is hereby
amended so that it reads as follows:

For the privilege of occupancy in any hotel, each transient shall be subject to and shall
pay a tax in the amount of ten-(T-Q-%) twelve (12%) percent of the rent charged by the
operator. Such tax shall constitute a debt owed by the transient to the City, which debt
shall be extinguished only by payment to the operator or to the City. The transient shall
pay the tax to the operator of the hotel at the time the rent is paid. If the rent is paid in
installments, a proportionate share of the tax shall be paid with each installment. The
unpaid tax shall be due upon the transient's ceasing to occupy space in the hotel. If for
any reason the tax due is not paid to the operator of the hotel, the Tax Administrator may
require that such tax shall be paid directly to the Tax Administrator.

Section 2. This ordinance amending Section 3-7.03 of Chapter 7 of Title 3 of the Pacifica
Municipal Code shall be in full force and effect ten (10) days after the date on which the City
Council has approved the canvass of votes and declared that the voters of the City of Pacifica
have approved said ordinance by a vote of no less than a majority of the votes cast by the electors
voting on the tax measure set forth in this ordinance at the general municipal election to be held
on Tuesday, November 2, 2010. The effective date for purposes of levying the increased tax shall
be July 1, 2011.

Section 3. The City Clerk shall publish this ordinance according to law.

The foregoing ordinance was introduced on June 28, 2010, and passed and adopted at a regular
meeting of the City Council of the City of Pacifica held on 12th day of July, 2010, for submission
to the City voters at an election to be held on November 2, 2010, by the following vote:
