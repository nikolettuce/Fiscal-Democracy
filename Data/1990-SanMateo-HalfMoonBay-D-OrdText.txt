ORDINANCE NO.__________

ORDINANCE OF THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF
HALF MOON BAY AMENDING SECTION 3.12.030 THE HALF MOON
BAY MUNICIPAL CODE INCREASING THE TRANSIENT OCCUPANCY
TAX FOR HOTEL/MOTEL OCCUPANTS

IT IS HEREBY ORDAINED by the City Council of the City of
Half Moon Bay as follows:

SECTION 1:

A.	Section 3.12.030 of the Half Moon Bay Municipal
Code is hereby amended by modifying the transient
occupancy tax stated from eight (8%) percent to ten (10%)
percent.

B.	In all other respects there shall be no
modification of said Section 3.12.030.

SECTION 2:	The City Council finds that the transient

occupancy tax has not been increased since 1978. In
addition, the transient occupancy tax rate charged is
not consistent with rates charged by other local coastal
cities in Northern California.

SECTION 3:	That the City Council hereby declares that

it would have passed this Ordinance sentence by sentence, paragraph
by paragraph, and section by section, and does hereby declare that
any provisions on this Ordinance are severable and, if for any
