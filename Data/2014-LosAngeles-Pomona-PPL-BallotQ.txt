Ballot question

The question on the ballot:[i]

To provide revenue dedicated solely for the Pomona Public Library, shall
$42.00 per parcel be assessed commencing 7/1/2015? Restores Library
hours to 38 hours/week minimum and provides for professional staff.
Assessment expires in 10 years. Revenue raised is additional to monies
from City’s General Fund. Ensures City’s annual contribution to Library
remains no less than current general fund allocations. Revenue use is
subject to annual audit and citizen oversight. [2]
