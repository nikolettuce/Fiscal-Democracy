RESOLUTION NO. 2014-146

A RESOLUTION OF THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF HOLLISTER,
CALIFORNIA, ORDERING THE SUBMISSION TO THE QUALIFIED ELECTORS OF
THE CITY OF A CERTAIN MEASURE RELATING TO UNIFORM TRANSIENT
OCCUPANCY TAX (TOT) AT THE GENERAL ELECTION TO BE HELD ON
TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 4, 2014, AS CALLED BY RESOLUTION NO. 2014-130 AND
CONSOLIDATED BY RESOLUTION NO. 2014-131

WHEREAS, a General Election on Tuesday, November 4, 2014, has been called
by Resolution No. 2014-130, adopted on June 16, 2014; and

WHEREAS, the City Council of the City of Hollister also desires to submit to the
voters at the election a question relating to Uniform Transient Occupancy Tax (TOT);

NOW, THEREFORE, THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF HOLLISTER,
CALIFORNIA, DOES RESOLVE, DECLARE, DETERMINE AND ORDER AS
FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. That the City Council, pursuant to its right and authority,
does order submitted to the voters at the General Municipal Election the following
question:

Shall the Uniform Transient Occupancy Tax be
increased to twelve percent (12%)?

SECTION 2. That the proposed measure submitted to the voters is
attached as “Exhibit A”.

SECTION 3. That in alt particulars not recited in this Resolution, the
election shall be held and conducted as provided by law for holding municipal elections.

SECTION 4. That notice of the time and place of holding the election is
given and the City Clerk is authorized, instructed and directed to give further or
additional notice of the election, in time, form and manner as required by law.

"Exhibit A"

ORDINANCE NO. 1101

AN ORDINANCE OF THE CITY OF HOLLISTER
AMENDING HOLLISTER MUNICIPAL CODE
CHAPTER 3.32.030 - UNIFORM TRANSIENT OCCUPANCY TAX

The City Council of the City of Hollister hereby ordains as follows:

SECTION 1. CODE AMENDMENT. Section 3.32.030 Tax Imposed is amended as
follows:

The first sentence of Section 3.32.030 Tax imposed is amended to read;

For the privilege of occupancy in any hotel, each transient shall be subject
to and shall pay a tax in the amount of twelve (12%) percent of the rent
charged by the operator.

SECTION 2. SEVERABILITY If any provision of this ordinance or the application
thereof to any person or circumstance is held invalid by a court of
competent jurisdiction, the remainder of the ordinance and the application
of such provision to other persons or other circumstances shall not be
affected thereby.

SECTION 3. CODIFICATION. Upon adoption of this ordinance pursuant to the voter
approval, the City Clerk, is hereby authorized and directed to codify this
ordinance in the Hollister Municipal Code.

SECTION 4. EFFECTIVE DATE. This Ordinance, following its approval by a two-third
(2/3) majority of the City Council and its publication, shall become effective
upon the approval of the tax imposed hereunder by a majority of the
voters of the City voting thereon at the election to be held November 4,
2014. Pursuant to Elections Code Section 9217, this ordinance shall
become effective ten (10) days after the Council declares the results of the
election.

SECTION 5. VOTE OF THE PUBLIC. The City Council finds and declares that this
ordinance was passed by a majority of the City's qualified electors at an
