CITY OF OAKDALE

ANALYSIS OF CITY MEASURE ON BALLOT

The following analysis of the ballot measure for the general
election for the City of Oakdale on _ November 6,	1990, has been

prepared by the City Attorney's office.

Measure 'I:. Shall an ordinance amending Section 11-14 of the
Code of the City of Oakdale, providing that "for the privilege
and occupancy in any hotel, each transient is subject and shall
pay a tax in the amount of 10% of the rent charged by the
operator", be adopted? If Measure I passes, the transit
occupancy tax rate as set forth in Section 11-14 of the Oakdale
City Code would be increased from 7% to 10%.

Respectfully submitted.
