Ballot question

The following question appeared on the ballot:[i]

To maintain and improve general city services including fast response to
9-1-1 emergencies/fires, adding police officers/neighborhood patrols/youth
services to reduce gang activity/drug-related crimes, repairing potholes,
and maintaining local streets/parks/facilities/infrastructure, shall NO City of
Madera establish an ongoing 1/2 cent sales tax until ended by voters,
providing approximately $3.5 million annually, requiring independent
audits and all funds controlled locally? [2]
