Sonoma County Library
Measure Y
November 8, 2016

Ballot Question:

To maintain, restore/enhance Library services including: maintaining children's/teen
books, materials/services; providing educational services, including homework
help/computer labs; expanding senior/disabled services; keeping qualified librarians;
restoring library hours; shall the Sonoma County Library increase the sales tax by
1/8 of a percent, providing $12,000,000 annually for ten years, and increase the
spending limit to allow use of the revenue, with independent audits, citizen oversight,
requiring all funds go to Sonoma County Library/no money for Sacramento?
