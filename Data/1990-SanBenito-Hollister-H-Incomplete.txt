ARGUMENT IN FAVOR OF MEASURE H

ARGUMENTO A FAVOR DE LA MEDIOA H

Police and Fire Services are vital to the quality of life in Hollister, Crime is
increasing and the City cannot afford to hire the police staff needed to
adequately deal with this problem. Additional firemen are necessary for
prevention and to insure rapid response to emergencies.

Over the last ten years, Hollister’s population had doubled and dramatically
increased in area. During the same period, principal revenue sources used to
provide public safety services have not kept pace and, consequently, police
and fire manpower has stayed at the pre-1979 level or fallen below that level.

Drug related crimes - drug sales, theft, assaults and other violent crimes
are up because the City doesn’t have enough police officers on the streets.
Measure H will help deal with this problem, A correlation between crime
levels and police manning is very dear in our neighboring communities, and
we deserve to benefit from better public safety services.

Public safety makes up over 44% of the City budget. Without Measure H,
the City cannot increase public safety services. The City frequently loses
trained personnel to nearby communities with better compensation and
working conditions. Continuous turnover and undermanning create high
stress and dangerous situations during responses. Measure H will help keep
qualified public safety staff in Hollister.

The Special Public Safety Tax, now set at a low level, and which can never
exceed $150.00 per home a year is fair and equitable. All Hollister citizens wiii
benefit immediately, and continue to benefit for years to come.

Measure H means Help - Helping our community be a safer and better
place to live. Helping our children grow up in a drug free community. Helping
ail of us because we know that, when we need help, it will be there. Please
help keep Hollister safe by voting Yes for Measure H.

Dated: March 11,1990

s/ Hugh R. Riley, City Manager
