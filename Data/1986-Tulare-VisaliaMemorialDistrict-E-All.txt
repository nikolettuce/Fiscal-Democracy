REMODELING AND REPAIRING BUILDING AND/OR GROUNDS

Shall the Visalia Memorial District be authorized to levy a special tax, for a period of three (3) years beginning
on July 1,1987, upon all persons owning parcels of land within the Visalia Memorial District, at the rate of Three
Dollars ($3.00) per parcel?

For the purposes of this measure, no person shall pay more than six (6} dollars in any calendar year.

1,	The proceeds of this special tax shall be used solely and exclusively for the purpose of purchasing insurance
and for the remodeling and repairing of the Buifding, including but not limited to the replacement of part of the
roof and floors, the replacement of the stage and window curtains, the renovation of the kitchen, including the
purchase and installation of three new stoves, a hood, and a garbage disposal and a fire protection system, and
the repair and painting of the woodwork.

2,	The Board of Directors of the Visalia Memorial District may cancel or reduce any or all of the special tax
at any time or for limited periods of time during the life of the tax prior to the expiration of the three-year period
if the District receives sufficed! funds for the above-mentioned purposes.

IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY COUNTY COUNSEL
MEASUREE

In 1979, the Legislature added sections'50075-50077 to the Government Code to implement the provisions
of Article Xlll-A of the California Constitution. These statutes authorize cities, counties and districts to submit to
the voters for approval or rejection a measure authorizing the levying of a special tax.

if two-thirds of the qualified electors voting on the measure vote for approval, the Board of Directors of the
Visalia Memorial District will be authorized to levy an annual special tax upon ail parcels of land located within
the boundaries of the Visalia Memorial District. The annual tax shall be at the rate of three dollars ($3,00) per
parcel with no person paying more Than six dollars ($8,00) in any calendar year. This special tax will remain
in effect for a period of three years and the Board of Directors may cancel or reduce any or all of the special
tax at any time or for limited periods of time prior to the expiration of the three year period. The proceeds of
the tax shall only be used for the purpose of purchasing insurance and remodeling and repairing the Memorial
District building and grounds located in the City of Visalia.

If two-thirds of the qualified electors voting on the measure do not vote for approval, the measure wilt fail
and the proposed special tax will not be levied.

Except as noted above, this special tax would have no effect on the existing law. The electors who will be
entitled to vote on the measure will be those qualified electors residing within the boundaries of the Visalia Memorial
District.
