CITY OF MADERA
UTILITY USERS TAX ADVISORY MEASURE

The City Council of the City of Madera is submitting to the voters of the
City a request for an advisory vote on whether or not the City should levy
a tax of not more than five percent (5%) on certain utilities (gas. electric,
cable TV). The Council already has authority to levy such a tax and conse-
quently a favorable vote on the measure will not necessarily result in the
imposition of a tax, nor will its failure to carry by a majority of the vote
prohibit the Council from imposing such a tax. As stated, because the measure
is advisory only, it has no effect upon existing law.

s/ Axel E. Christiansen
City Attorney
