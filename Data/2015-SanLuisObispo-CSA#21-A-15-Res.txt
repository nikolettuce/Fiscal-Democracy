RESOLUTION NO. 2015-24

RESOLUTION CREATING A ZONE FOR ROAD IMPROVEMENTS
WITHIN THE SAN LUIS OBISPO COUNTY SERVICE AREA NO. 21

FOR LEONARD PLACE

RECITALS:

The following resolution is now offered and read:

WHEREAS, the majority of residents and property owners within the proposed “Road
Improvement Zone - Leonard Place,” area map attached hereto as Exhibit “A,” have requested road
improvement services through the formation of a zone for the implementation of the County’s
Cooperative Improvement Program; and

WHEREAS, the residents and property owners desire, and it is in the public interest that said
zone be established; and

WHEREAS, the Road Improvement Zone - Leonard Place is entirely within the boundary of
County Service Area No. 21; and

WHEREAS, Section 25217 of the Government Code authorizes the Board of Supervisors to
establish a zone within any county service area; and

WHEREAS, the purpose of establishing said Road Improvement Zone - Leonard Place is to
impose, levy and collect a special tax on the real property therein to pay the costs to construct road
improvement services within said Zone; and

WHEREAS, all proposed tax levies within the proposed Road Improvement Zone - Leonard
Place are commensurate with the special services to be provided within said Zone; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors on December 16, 2014, adopted Resolution 2014-358
calling for a January 27, 2015, public hearing on forming the Road Improvement Zone - Leonard Place
within County Service Area No. 21; and

WHEREAS, a public hearing on the question of forming the Road Improvement Zone - Leonard
Place of County Service Area No. 21 was to be held on January 27, 2015, in the Supervisor’s
Chambers, San Luis Obispo, California; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors finds it to be in the public interest to create the Road
Improvement Zone - Leonard Place in County Service Area No. 21.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ORDERED by the Board of Supervisors of the
County of San Luis Obispo, State of California, as follows:

1.	That the recitals set forth herein above are true, correct, and valid.

2.	That there be and hereby is established and formed within San Luis Obispo County
Service Area No. 21, a new zone with boundaries as shown on
Exhibit “A” attached hereto.

3.	The new zone shall be named “Road Improvement Zone - Leonard Place.”

4.	That within said zone the following services are to be provided, when authorized and
funded by this Board of Supervisors:

• The extended service of road improvement of public rights of way.

Upon motion of Supervisor Gibson, seconded by Supervisor Hill, and on the following roll call
vote, to wit:

AYES:	Superivsors	Gibson,	Hill,	Compton, Arnold, and Chairperson Mecham

NOES:	None

ABSENT:	None

ABSTAINING: None

the foregoing Resolution is hereby adopted on the 27th day of January, 2015.
