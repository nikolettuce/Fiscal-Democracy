Ballot question

The question on the ballot appeared as:[i]

To improve our quality of life, maintain and enhance city services and
facilities, including: crime and gang prevention; neighborhood policing and
school safety; safety inspections; police, fire and paramedic response;
fixing potholes, streets, and sidewalks; recreation and programs for youth
and seniors; and other city services, shall the City of Salinas enact a one
cent sales tax, that can’t be taken by the State, with citizens’ oversight,
annual independent audits, with all funds dedicated to Salinas? [2]
