RESOLUTION NO. 04-30

A RESOLUTION OF THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF CALEXICO
CALIFORNIA, RECITING THE FACT OF THE GENERAL MUNICIPAL
ELECTION HELD ON TUESDAY, MARCH 2,2004 DECLARING THE RESULT
AND SUCH OTHER MATTERS AS PROVIDED BY LAW.

WHEREAS, a General Municipal Election was held and conducted in the City of
Calexico, California, on Tuesday March 2, 2004 as required by law; and

WHEREAS, notice of the election was given in time, form and manner as
provided by law; that voting precincts were properly established; that election officers
were appointed and that in all respects the election was held and conducted and the votes
were cast, received and canvassed and returns made and declared in time, form and
manner as required by the provisions of the (Elections Code of the State of California for
the holding of elections in general law cities); and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Resolution No. 03-64 adopted on the 8th day of October
2003, the Calexico City Council requested consolidation of Elections and Election
Services of the County Clerk for March 2, 2004.

NOW, THEREFORE, THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF
CALEXICO, CALIFORNIA, DOES RESOLVE, DECLARE, DETERMINE AND
ORDER AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. That the whole number of ballots cast in the City except absent
voter ballots was 2,915.

That the whole number of absent voter ballots cast in the City were 937, making a
total of 3,852 ballots cast in the City.

SECTION 2. That the names of persons voted for at the election for Member of

SECTION 3. That the number of votes given in the City to each of the persons
above named for the respective office for which the persons were candidates (and for and
against the measures are listed in Exhibit “A” attached).

SECTION 4. The City Council does declare and determine that Lewis Pacheco
was elected as Member of the City Council for the full term of four years, that John R.
Renison was re-elected as Member of the City Council for the full term of four years and
that Carmen M. Durazo was elected as Member of the City Council for the full term of
four years. Also that Lourdes Cordova was re-elected as City Clerk for a full term of four
years and that Mr. Rodolfo Moreno was re-elected as City Treasurer for a full term of
four years.

That as a result of the election, a majority of the voters voted against the measure
relating to Measure “H” which would adopt Ordinance 1005 amending section 3.24.020
of the Calexico Municipal Code to increase the City’s transient occupancy tax from 7% to
10%.

SECTION 5. The City Clerk shall enter on the records of the City Council of the
City of Calexico, a statement of the result of the election, showing: 1) The whole number
of ballots cast in the City; 2) The names of persons voted for; 3) The measures voted
upon; 4) For what office each person was voted for; 5) The number of votes given to each
person, and or against each measure.

SECTION 6. That the City Clerk shall immediately make and deliver to each of
the persons so elected a Certificate of Election signed by the City Clerk and
authenticated; that the City Clerk shall also administer to each person elected the Oath of
Office prescribed in the Constitution of the State of California and shall have them
subscribe to it and file it in the office of the City Clerk. Each and all of the persons so
elected shall then be inducted into the respective office to which they have been elected.

SECTION 7. That the City Clerk shall certify to the passage and adoption of this
resolution and enter it into the book of original resolutions.
