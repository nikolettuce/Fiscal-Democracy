November 6

, 1990 MEASURE D - MARINWOOD COMMUNITY SERVICES DISTRACT

SPECIAL PARAMEDIC SERVICES TAX

Shall the ordinance of the Board of Directors of the
Marinwood Community Services District imposing a special
tax for paramedic services of not to exceed thirty-five
dollars(35,000)per year for each living unit as a basic
charge be confirmed and the appropriations limit be
increased by an amount equal to the proceeds of said
tax for each of the next four (4)years?

NEEDED	YES	NO	RESULT

2/3	1,345	535	PASSED
