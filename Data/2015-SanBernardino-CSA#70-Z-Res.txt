RESOLUTION NO. 2015-84

RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF THE COUNTY OF SAN BERNARDINO,
STATE OF CALIFORNIA, ACTING AS THE GOVERNING BODY OF COUNTY SERVICE AREA 70,
ZONE P-6 (El MIRAGE), AUTHORIZING AN ELECTION FOR AN INCREASE IN THE SPECIAL

TAX FOR RECREATION AND PARK SERVICES

On Tuesday June 2, 2015, on motion of Supervisor Lovingood, duly seconded by Supervisor
Hagman and carried, the following resolution is adopted by the Board of Supervisors of San
Bernardino County, State of California.

SECTION 1. The Board of Supervisors of the County of San Bernardino, acting in its
capacity as the governing body of County Service Area 70, Zone P-6, hereby finds and determines:

(a)	That County Service Area 70, Zone P-6 (CSA 70 P-6) was established on October 15,
1990 with a special tax of $9.00 per parcel after registered voters approved a ballot measure for the
provision of recreation and park services.

L

(b)	That this Board desires to authorize an election to increase the special tax from the
current $9.00 per parcel to an increase to $12.00 per parcel with an annual inflationary increase of
3.75% thereafter for the provision of recreation and park services.

SECTION 2. The Board of Supervisors of the County of San Bernardino, acting in its
capacity as the governing body of County Service Area 70, Zone P-6, therefore, hereby resolves and
orders:

(a)	That this Board hereby authorizes an election to propose an increase in the special tax
from the current $9.00 per parcel to an increase to $12.00 per parcel with an annual inflationary
increase of 3.75% thereafter for the provision of recreation and park services.

(b)	That this Board authorizes a special tax election to be voted on by registered voters
within CSA 70 P-6 through a mailed ballot election conducted by the County Register of Voters
Election Office.

(c)	That the election date for the proposed ballot measure is September 1, 2015.

(d)	That the proposed special tax will be placed on the property tax bill for collection by the
Auditor-Controller/Treasurer/Tax Collector if the election is successful.

PASSED AND ADOPTED by the Board of Supervisors of the County of San Bernardino, State

of California, by the following vote:
