June 2, 1992

CONSOLIDATED PRIMARY ELECTION

MEASURE D - SPECIAL TAX FOR PARAMEDIC SERVICES

Shall the ordinance of the Board of Supervisors of County
Service Area No. 28 imposing a special tax for paramedic,
services not to exceed Twenty-five Dollars ($25.00) per
year for each living unit, as a basic charge, in fiscal
year 1992-1993, Thirty Dollars ($30.00) per living unit
for fiscal year 1993-1994, Thirty-five Dollars ($35.00)
per living unit for fiscal year 1994-1995, and Forty
Dollars ($40.00) per living unit for fiscal year 1995-1996,
be confirmed, and the appropriations limit of County Service
Area No. 28 be adopted and established in an amount equal to
the proceeds of such taxes?

VOTED NEEDED	YES	NO	RESULT

1,115

2/3

3,075

Passed
