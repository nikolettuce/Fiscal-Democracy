RESOLUTION NO. 243-2014

OF THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF THE COUNTY OF EL DORADO

MANY OAKS LANE ZONE OF BENEFIT #98132 WITHIN COUNTY SERVICE AREA #9 - SETTING
FOR MAILED BALLOT ELECTION A MEASURE TO CONSIDER A SPECIAL TAX FOR THE
PURPOSES OF PROVIDING ROAD IMPROVEMENT AND MAINTENANCE SERVICES AND
DRAINAGE FACILITY MAINTENANCE SERVICES

WHEREAS, the Board of Supervisors of the County of El Dorado has formed County Service Area #9
pursuant to Title 3, Division 2, Part 2, Chapter 2.2 of the California Government Code; and

WHEREAS, this Board formed Many Oaks Lane Zone of Benefit #98132 (Zone) on May 27, 1986 by
Resolution 135-86 for the purpose of providing road improvement and maintenance services and drainage
facility maintenance services; and

WHEREAS, California Government Code §25215.2, together with §25217.4, expressly authorizes a zone
within a county service area to levy a special tax for road improvement and maintenance services subject to
voter approval; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to California State Law, the Board of Supervisors is required to place before the
electorate the approval of the measure as shown on Exhibit A before the special tax can be levied; and

WHEREAS, the County Registrar of Voters has determined that one hundred ninety-two registered voters
reside within the zone of benefit boundaries; and

WHEREAS, such a special tax shall take effect only if approved by a two-thirds majority of the voters
casting a ballot in the special tax ballot proceeding; and

WHEREAS, the zone advisory committee has determined that an annual special tax of $250.00 per parcel of
land, whether improved or unimproved, regardless of use, is reasonable and sufficient to provide road
improvement and maintenance services and drainage facilty maintenance and improvement services within
the zone of benefit for fiscal year 2015/2016 and thereafter.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED the Board of Supervisors determines as follows:

1.	The measure as shown on Exhibit A shall be submitted to the voters of the Many Oaks Lane
Zone of Benefit #98132 within County Service Area #9.

2.	The El Dorado County Registrar of Voters shall submit the measure as shown on Exhibit A to
the electorate in a mailed ballot election to be held on May 5, 2015.

3.	The tax amount as shown on Exhibit A, if approved, shall appear as a separate item on the tax
bill of each affected parcel and shall be collected at the same time and manner as county ad
valorem property taxes are collected as set forth in the applicable provisions of the Revenue
and Taxation Code, and shall be subject to the same penalties and the same procedure and sale
in the case of delinquency as provided for such taxes. All laws applicable to the levy, collection
and enforcement of county property taxes shall be applicable to such tax.

4.	The special tax is subject to the accountability measures required by State Law, including

5.	The special tax amount shown on Exhibit A shall take effect and be imposed only if it is
