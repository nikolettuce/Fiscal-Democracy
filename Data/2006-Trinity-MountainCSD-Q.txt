NOTICE OF MEASURE P
AND NOTICE OF DATE FIXED FOR ARGUMENTS TO BE SUBMITTED

FOR OR AGAINST MEASURE

NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that an election will be held for the voters to approve
Twenty-Four Hour Medical Service Rapid Access Measure within the area identified by
LAFCO on TUESDAY, November 7, 2006, to submit to the voters the following measure:

MEASURE P

“TWENTY-FOUR HOUR EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICE RAPID ACCESS
MEASURE. To provide Trinity County residents access to basic emergency medical
services, with a doctor on duty 24-hours a day, shall the Mountain Community Medical
Services District levy, for five years, an annual interim special parcel tax within its
boundaries as follows: $32 per undeveloped parcel, $118 per improved nonresidential
parcel, $118 per dwelling unit, in accordance with LAFCo resolution 06-03, and provide
for guaranteed annual performance and financial accountability?

YES

NO

NOTICE IS HEREBY FURTHER GIVEN that written arguments may be submitted to the
Trinity County Clerk for or against the measure.

NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN that the arguments shall not exceed 300 words in length,
and must be submitted to the County Clerk at his office at 11 Court Street, P.O. Box
1215, Weaverville, CA 96093 no later than 5:00 p.m. on Friday, August 18, 2006.
Arguments that have been submitted may be changed up until the filing deadline on
August 18, 2006.

NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN that, if more than one argument for or more than one
argument against each measure is submitted by the filing deadline, the County Clerk will
select one argument for and one argument against each measure to be printed and
distributed to the voters, and he will send a copy of the argument in favor of each
measure to the author(s) of the argument against each measure, and a copy of the
argument against each measure to the author(s) of the argument in favor of each
measure. The authors may prepare and submit rebuttal arguments not to exceed 250
words in length. Rebuttal arguments must be filed no later than 5:00 p.m. on Monday,
September 4, 2006.

NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN that the Measure election be held with the Statewide
General Election. Ballots must be received by the County Clerk by 8:00 p.m. on
Tuesday, November 7, 2006.

DERO B. FORSLUND
County Clerk/Registrar of Voters
Dated: Friday, August 11, 2006
