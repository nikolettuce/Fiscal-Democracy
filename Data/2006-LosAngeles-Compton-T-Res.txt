AN ORDINANCE OF THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF
COMPTON CALLING AND GIVING NOTICE OF A SPECIAL
ELECTION TO BE CONSOLIDATED WITH THE GENERAL
ELECTION TO BE HELD ON TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 7, 2006,

FOR THE SUBMISSION OF A MEASURE TO THE QUALIFIED
ELECTORATE OF THE CITY OF COMPTON

WHEREAS, the City Council desires to submit the following question to the
voters at the Special 1 Election to be consolidated with the General Election:
whether the City shall reduce its Utility Users’ Tax on Telecommunications
Services.

NOW, THEREFORE, THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF
COMPTON, CALIFORNIA, DOES ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

Section 1. That notice is hereby given that the City Council of the City of
Compton is hereby calling for and giving notice of a special election to be held on
November 7, 2006.

Section 2. That a Special Election will be consolidated with the General
Election for the County of Los Angeles to be held on November 7, 2006, and

Section 3. That pursuant to the requirements of the City Charter, there is
called and ordered to be held in the City of Compton, California on Tuesday,
November 7, 2006, a Special Election for the purpose of submitting the following
Measure.

MEASURE

Shall an ordinance be adopted that would
reduce the City of Compton’s current utility
users tax on telecommunication services
from 10% to 8.5% and modernize the
telecommunications services definitions so
that all taxpayers are treated in the same
manner?

Section 4. That the cost of said services, upon the completion thereof,
shall be a proper charge against the City.

Section 5. That this ordinance shall take effect immediately upon adoption
in accordance with Section 611 of the Compton City Charter.

Section 6. That the City Clerk shall certify the adoption of the ordinance
and shall forward certified copies hereof, to the Clerk of the Board of Supervisors,
and to the Registrar Recorder/County Clerk of the County of Los Angeles.
