ARGUMENT IN FAVOR OF MEASURE 0

Are you willing to invest a quarter to protect the Napa countryside? Your ap-
proval ot Measure 0 will help save wildlife habitat, protect the Napa River, and
keep hillsides free from development.

Measure 0 provides for the levy of a 1/4 of one percent sales tax on those
few items that are taxable. This means that for every $100 you pay for taxable
items, you invest a quarter in open space. You will not pay any tax on food,
rent, mortgage payments, gasoline, utilities, medical expenses, insurance, repairs
and services because these are not taxable items.

Measure 0 provides the means by which Measure N can protect our country-
