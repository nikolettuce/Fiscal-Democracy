ORDINANCE OF THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF BRAWLEY AMENDING
THE BRAWLEY MUNICIPAL CODE RELATING TO ITS SALES AND USE TAX

THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF BRAWLEY DOES ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. Section 24.6 of Article II, Chapter 24 of the Brawley Municipal Code is hereby
amended to read as follows:

Section 24.6. Imposition and rate of tax.

For the privilege of selling tangible personal property at retail, a tax is hereby imposed
upon all retailers at the rate of one and one-half per cent of the gross receipts of any
retailer from the sale of all tangible personal property sold at retail in the City on or after
the effective date hereof. The license tax imposed by the provisions of this section is in
addition to any and all other taxes imposed by this Code or other ordinance of the City,
Ten years from the effective date hereof, the rate shall return to one percent.

hereby amended to read as follows:

Section 24.25. Imposition and rate of tax.

An excise tax is hereby imposed on the use or other consumption in the City of tangible
personal property purchased from any retailer on or after the effective date hereof for use
or other consumption in the City at the rate of one and one-half percent of the sales price
of the property. Ten years from the effective date hereof, the rate shall return to one percent.

the date of its adoption and prior to the expiration of fifteen (15) days from the passage
thereof, shall be published at least once in the Imperial Valley Press, a newspaper of
general circulation, printed and published in the County of Imperial, State of California,
together with the names of the members of the City Council voting for and against the
same.

IMPARTIAL ANALYSIS BY CITY ATTORNEY
PROPOSED SALES TAX INCREASE MEASURE W
City of Brawley

On November 6,2007, the voters of the City of Brawley will be asked to approve an increase
to the sales tax imposed in the City of Brawley by one-half of one percent. The impact of
the increase would amount to an additional fifty cents for each one hundred dollars of
taxable sales.

The stated purpose of the increase is to “...expand, develop, preserve and protect the
safety and character of the City of Brawley with a priority on funding essential public services
such as police, fire, crime prevention and street and park maintenance as well as other
City services...". By the express terms of the ballot measure, the increased sales tax will
sunset after ten years.

The increase is a general tax, A majority vote in favor of this will mean that the sales fax
collected in the City of Brawley will increase by one-half of one percent for a period of ten
years from its effective date after which the one-half of one percent increase will end.

ANALISIS IMPARCIAL POR EL ABOGADO MUNICIPAL
EL PROPUESTO AUMENTO AL IMPUESTO DE VENTAS DE LA MEDIDA W
Ciudad de Brawley

El 6 de noviembre, 2007, se le va a pedir a los votantes de la Ciudad de Brawley que
aprueben un aumento al impuesto de ventas que se impone en la Ciudad de Brawley por
la mitad de un por ciento. El impacto del aumento montaria a cincuenta centavos adicionales
por cien dolares de ventas impositivas.

El proposito declarado del aumento es el de“..,aumentar, desarrollar, preservar y proteger
la seguridad y el caracter de la Ciudad de Brawley con una prioridad en costear servicios
publicos esenciales tales como policia, bomberos, prevencion del crimen y mantenimiento
de calles y parques lo mismo que otros servicios municipales...” De acuerdo a la
terminologia express de esta medida en la boleta, el aumento al impuesto de ventas se
borrara despues de diez anos.

El aumento es un impuesto general. Un voto de mayoria a favor de esto quiere decir que
el impuesto de venta cobrado en la Ciudad de Brawley va a aumentar por la mitad de un
por ciento por un periodo de diez anos de su fecha efectiva, y despues de esa techa el
aumento de la mitad de un por ciento se terminara.

f/ Dennis H. Morita, Abogado Municipal



ARGUMENT IN FAVOR OF MEASURE W

The City of Brawley has always meant “QUALITY OF LIFE". We enjoy municipal services
such as police, fire, crime prevention, street and park maintenance, and library that are
second to none. The unbelievable success of the sports programs at the high school can
trace its roots to the numerous City recreation programs,

Maintaining the services and programs that set our City apart from the rest and make
Brawley a great place to live, work and raise a family is becoming increasingly costly. Jusi
a few short years ago, a house costing a quarter of a million dollars would have been a
mansion instead of just “affordable”.

We do not propose this increase to the sales tax lightly. We recognize that although the
increase is small; fifty cents on one hundred dollars of taxable sales, we take very seriously
our obligation to the citizens of Brawley. Our vision for Brawley involves more than
maintaining the status quo. If we are to expand, develop and provide for new services.
this increase will be a vital component to accomplishing that goal.

The council has already established a framework for establishment of an oversight
committee to make recommendations to the Council regarding the manner in which
proceeds generated by the sales tax would be best utilized. The ultimate safeguard is the
fact that the increase sunsets after ten years.That is, the increase automatically terminates
in ten years.

This measure will assist in Brawley’s continued economic growth which provides for the
creation of job opportunities for our community.

Brawley has always pulled together to accomplish great things. Let’s pull together on
November 6. Vote yes on Measure W.
